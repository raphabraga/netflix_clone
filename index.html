GAME OF THRONES

In a land where the summers can last for a lot of years and the winter for a whole life, seven noble families fight for the control of a mystical land of Westeros, divided after a war. In a scenario which reminds a medieval Europe, kings, queens, knights and bastards use all the possible means in a political game for the clash for the Iron Throne, the symbol of absolute power.
